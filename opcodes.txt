opcode - operation selection

0000 - addition
0001 - substraction
0010 - multiplication
0011 - division
0100 - arithmetic and
0101 - arithmetic or
0110 - arithmetic xor
0111 - complement
1000 - leftshift output by 1
1001 - rightshift output by 1
1010 - rotate left output by 1
1011 - rotate right output by 1
1100 - greater than 
1101 - equalilty

1111 - no op
